What is TrollingValues?

TrollingValues is a light-hearted spoof of the political compass test which measures your political positions within the context of BMC. At the end, you will get a Political Compass result with a custom compass.

* This test is inspired by Ark Mapping's Ryan Compass and the Sapply Values test.
